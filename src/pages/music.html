New Music

Linger & Lurk

Check out my latest composition 'Linger and Lurk' on Soundcloud. This song falls in the ilk of such songs as Stevie Wonder's Creepin' as it is about the haunting visits for people from your past via dreams. Stylistically this song is inspired by any of the many wonderful tunes by Kenny Rankin.
